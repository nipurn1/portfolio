    MY RESPONSIBILITIES AS BDA
    As business development professional I had three primary responsibilities
    1.Identifying new sales leads
    2.Pitching products and/or services
    3.Maintaining fruitful relationships with existing customers
    When it comes to generating leads, My day-to-day duties typically include:
  • Researching organisations and individuals online (especially on social media) to identify new leads and potential new markets

  • Researching the needs of other companies and learning who makes decisions about purchasing

  • Contacting potential clients via email or phone to establish rapport and set up meetings

  • Planning and overseeing new marketing initiatives

  • Attending conferences, meetings, and industry events

    When it comes to the challenge of actually selling,My other typical duties include:
  • Preparing PowerPoint presentations and sales displays

  • Contacting clients to inform them about new developments in the company’s products

  • Developing quotes and proposals

  • Negotiating and renegotiating by phone, email, and in person

  • Developing sales goals for the team and ensuring they are met

  • Training personnel and helping team members develop their skills

To keep healthy relationships with clients, this mostly requires socialisation. So from simple chats on the phone to lunches and events or conferences I must be sure to keep my customers happy. Of course, as with all office jobs, documentation is also a big part of the work. I was also obligated to write reports and provide feedback to upper management about what is and is not working.

MY RESPONSIBILITIES AS SUDENT INTERN AT VCE

    In this role as a Civil Engineers, I need to select the appropriate material which will not produce much harm to the environment.
  • Reducing the wastage while construction

  • Using Eco friendly material for the building

  • Design accordingly that the building could use the natural ventilation properly

    • A ‘green’ building is a building that, in its design, construction or operation, reduces or eliminates negative impacts, and can create positive impacts, on our climate and natural environment. Green buildings preserve precious natural resources and improve our quality of life.
    There are a number of features which can make a building ‘green’. which I studied during my internship ,or also need to check on my projects ,These include:
  • Efficient use of energy, water and other resources

  • Use of renewable energy, such as solar energy

  • Pollution and waste reduction measures, and the enabling of re-use and recycling

  • Good indoor environmental air quality

  • Use of materials that are non-toxic, ethical and sustainable

  • Consideration of the environment in design, construction and operation

  • Consideration of the quality of life of occupants in design, construction and operation

  • A design that enables adaptation to a changing environment
